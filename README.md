# AsthmaModel
Dissertation Project -

This project aimed to develop a predictive model to help identify patients at risk of undiagnosed asthma or those who are at risk of asthma due to their environmental and lifestyle factors. The primary objectives included collecting datasets and enhancing them with additional variables such as BMI, Smoking status, Genetics, and environmental factors. Additionally, an Asthma risk scoring system was developed to categorize patients into different groups based on factors. The model was trained and validated achieving an average accuracy of 89%, the model correctly identified key predictors of asthma risk, like smoking status and air quality. The GUI was developed with healthcare usage in mind, making it friendly and an appropriate GUI to use in the workplace. The results from running tests were positive and they showed that patients were being correctly categorized. This paper highlights the development of the product and the feasibility of it in real world scenarios.
